Nectar & Root

Nectar & Root is a floral and bridal company in Vermont. I did a project with them a while back to practice my branding design and create a new brand for them. I created a new logo, a sample design of a brochure and business card designs.

Since Nectar & Root deals in flower arrangements, flowers had to be the start of the show. I was able to find high-quality photographs of flower bouquets, wedding photos and the like provided by them. Keeping in line with putting the spotlight on the flowers I created a brochure that both showcased their talent and beautiful designs but was balanced well with information about their company and what they did for their customers.

To accompany the elegance portrayed in the brochure I wanted the business cards to be just as delicate. I designed the business cards to be simple, to-the-point and dainty yet professional. I liked the idea of using a grainy material for the business cards to portray the earthiness of the outdoors and of flowers themselves.

I came up with the idea of bouquet table tags. When bouquets were placed on tables during an event, each one would come with a tag that labeled what flowers were involved in the bouquet as well as either historical or symbolic meaning attached to them. This brings a sense of entertainment (and education) to each bouquet and gives the viewer a higher sense of attachment as well.
